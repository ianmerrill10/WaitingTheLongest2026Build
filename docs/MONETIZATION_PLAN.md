These go live Day 1. Zero infrastructure, just links.

- **Tag:** `waitingthelon-20`

## Phase 2: Platform Revenue (Month 3-6)

### Stream 8: Sponsored Shelter Profiles
- **Price:** $29/month (Basic), $79/month (Featured), $149/month (Premium)
- **Basic:** Logo + contact info on shelter page
- **Featured:** Top placement in city search results, "Verified Partner" badge
- **Premium:** Custom shelter landing page, priority API quota (10K/day vs 1K)
- **Target:** 50 Basic + 20 Featured + 5 Premium = $3,200/month at Month 6

### Stream 9: Dog of the Week Sponsorships
- **Price:** $500–2,000/month (negotiated per sponsor)
- **What they get:** Dog of the Week email blast (grows with list), homepage hero, social posts
- **Who buys:** Pet food brands, insurance companies, training services
- **Target:** 2 sponsors/month = $1,500–4,000/month

### Stream 10: Premium Alerts — "Priority Adopter"
- **Price:** $4.99/month
- **What they get:** Instant SMS/email alerts for new dogs matching their breed + location filters
- **For free:** Daily digest only
- **Tech:** Supabase Edge Functions + Resend for email, Twilio for SMS
- **Target:** 500 subscribers = $2,495/month
